# Code of Conduct

**Document role**: Repository-only community guidance

**Sync behavior**: Never copied into target repositories

## Our Standard

Contributors and maintainers are expected to communicate respectfully, assume good intent, welcome constructive disagreement, and focus criticism on ideas and changes rather than people.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption, publication of private information, and sustained hostile behavior are not acceptable.

## Scope

This policy applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and other spaces where someone represents the project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove inappropriate content, close or lock discussions, reject contributions, or restrict participation when necessary.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers rather than opening a public issue. Maintainers should handle reports discreetly, avoid conflicts of interest, and respond proportionally.
